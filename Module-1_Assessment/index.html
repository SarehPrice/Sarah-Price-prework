About Me

I graduated from Grand View University with a BA in Graphic Journalism and minors in Interactive Media and English.

I’m a huge fan of UX and UI design and research, social design, as well as the book publishing industry, journalism, and history. As well as dogs and Japan, I will talk your ear off about those two subjects.

In my free time, I’m writing novels, designing book covers, reading, practicing kendo and archery, blogging, bookbinding, stargazing, volunteering, and designing jewelry.

Thank you for checking out my work.
